Richard Eldridge,

Your new wire bracket [device] has a great potential. There are many applications that it can assist electricians with. The ability of one strap to hold many more conductors within one strap is just one of them. The 16 NM cables or 10 MC cables that can be installed without derating can be valuable when an electrician has many circuits that are to be routed to the panel as homeruns. When only 2 fasteners are needed for these supports instead of multiple fasteners or staples the labor savings can be tremendous. If the material is concrete instead of wood this savings can be even greater. With the competitive market we are in at this time, the advantage of saving labor and many times material costs and still providing a neat and orderly installation is vital.

My name is C. David Moss. I am a senior electrical inspector for Dekalb County, GA. I am also a code and estimating instructor in this area. I have been in the electrical industry since 1969 and have always tried to remain current on the NEC and current products and practices for our industry. Your product should find many uses that will make it valuable to contractors familiar with it.
