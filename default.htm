 The Wire Bracket Device also allows cable to be installed in a neat, orderly and safe manner while saving time and money. The interior of the wire bracket device is rounded and smooth for pulling cable. Assemble custom raceways as cables are being pulled or install the wire bracket device after routing cables. Wire bracket devices can be added at any time and in any location for any cable that requires additional support.

Use of the wire bracket device significantly reduces structural damage to wood joists by limiting the number of holes bored through the joists in order to install cable. Installation of the wire bracket device reduces the number of staples installed. Installing fewer staples reduces the possibility of increased resistance when staples are installed too tight.
